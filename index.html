About Me

Padius Vutlhari Mashele is a versatile and driven IT professional with hands-on experience in Salesforce development, business analysis, and data analytics. She has developed a solid foundation in declarative app development using Salesforce tools such as Flow Builder, Lightning Experience, and Data Import Wizard, with a focus on streamlining onboarding processes and improving internal workflows.

Padius has worked across various industries including insurance and the technology sector, contributing to data-driven decision-making and operational improvements. Her background includes analyzing customer complaints using SQL and Power BI at Old Mutual, performing business analysis and prototyping at institutions like TUT and Geeks4Learning, and solving small business challenges during her time with UVU Africa.

She is passionate about leveraging both technical and analytical skills to create efficient, user-focused solutions. Known for being a quick learner with strong attention to detail, Padius is comfortable working across multiple platforms, including Salesforce, SQL Server, Excel, and Qualtrics.
